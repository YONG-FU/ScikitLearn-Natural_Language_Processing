Applicable Margin means (i) prior to the occurrence of a Rate Decrease Event (if any), 8.75% per annum and (ii) on and after the occurrence of a Rate Decrease Event (if any), 7.00%  per  annum;  provided  that  in  the  event  that  a  Rate  Decrease  Event  occurs  during  an Interest  Period,  such  Rate  Decrease  Event  shall  be  deemed  to  have  occurred  on,  and  the reduction in Applicable Margin shall not become effective until, the first day of the following Interest Period.
