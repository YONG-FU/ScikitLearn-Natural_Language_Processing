Applicable Margin means (i) with respect to Term Loans that are LIBOR Rate Loans, a percentage, per annum, equal to 7.625%, (ii) with respect to Term Loans that are Base Rate Loans, a percentage,  per  annum,  equal  to  6.625%,  (iii)  with  respect  to  Revolving  Loans  that  are  LIBOR  Rate Loans, a percentage, per annum, equal to 4.25% and (iv) with respect to Revolving Loans that are Base Rate Loans, a percentage, per annum, equal to 3.25%.
