If any one Lender exercises its rights under this Section 5.13 to charge a Market Disruption Margin or to restrict the types of Borrowings, the Borrowers may, at their option, upon 30 Business Days notice to the Administrative Agent, Operating Agent and any applicable Lender (which notice shall be irrevocable), repay to one or more of the Lenders charging a Market Disruption Margin in full such Lenders Loan outstanding together with accrued and unpaid interest on the principal amount so repaid up to the date of repayment, together with such Market Disruption Margin as may be applicable to the date of payment and the applicable Commitment of such Lender shall be cancelled. Any such repayment by the Borrowers shall not be subject to the Term Loan Prepayment Fee.
