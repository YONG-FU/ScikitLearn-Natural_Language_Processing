39 Section 2.06  Interest  on  Loans.    (a)    Subject  to  the  provisions  of  Section 2.07,  the Loans comprising each ABR Borrowing shall bear interest (computed on the basis of the actual number of days elapsed over a year of 365 or 366 days, as the case may be, and calculated from and  including  the  date of  such  Borrowing  to  but excluding  the  date of  repayment  thereof)  at  a rate per annum equal to the Alternate Base Rate plus the Applicable Margin.
