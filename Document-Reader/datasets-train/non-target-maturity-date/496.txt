"3.5.3.  If the Term Loan Facility is not sooner terminated as contemplated by this Agreement, then, notwithstanding anything contained in this Agreement to the contrary, the Term Loan Facility shall automatically terminate on the Termination Date (defined below). ""Termination Date"" means the earlier of: (a) the Maturity Date or (b) the date on which the principal amount of all outstanding Term Loans have been declared due and payable pursuant to Section 3.5.1. Upon the occurrence of the Termination Date, all Loan Obligations shall be immediately due and payable in full."
