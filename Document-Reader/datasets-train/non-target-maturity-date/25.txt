"Action if Other Event of Default.  If any Event of Default (other than any Event of Default described in clauses (a) through (d) of Section 9.1(h) with respect to the Company or AMH) shall  occur  for  any  reason,  whether  voluntary  or  involuntary,  and  be  continuing,  the  Administrative Agent, upon the direction of the Required Holders, shall by notice to Company declare all or any portion of  the  outstanding  principal  amount  of  the  Notes  and  other  Note  Obligations  to  be  due  and  payable without  further  notice,  demand  or  presentment,  whereupon  the  full  unpaid  amount  of  such  Notes  and other Note Obligations that shall be so declared due and payable shall be and become immediately due and payable."
