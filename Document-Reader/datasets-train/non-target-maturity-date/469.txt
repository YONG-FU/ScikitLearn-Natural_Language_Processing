"(b) Unused Line Fee.  On the first day of each month prior to the Maturity Date, Borrower shall pay to Agent, for the benefit of each Lender with a Revolver Commitment in accordance with each such Lender's Pro Rata Share of the Revolver Commitment, an unused line fee in an amount equal to 0.50% per annum times the result of (i) the Maximum Revolver Amount, less (ii) the sum of (A) the average Daily Balance of Advances that were outstanding during  the  immediately  preceding  month,  plus  (B) the  average  Daily  Balance  of  the  Letter  of Credit Usage during the immediately preceding month."
