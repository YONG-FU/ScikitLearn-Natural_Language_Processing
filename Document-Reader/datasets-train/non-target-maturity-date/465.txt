"(g) Termination of Revolver Facility.  Borrower shall have the right, at any  time  and  from  time  to  time,  to  terminate,  but  only  as  a  whole,  the  Revolving  Loan Commitment;  provided,  however,  Borrower  shall  immediately  prepay  in  full  all  Revolving Loans outstanding on the effective date of any such termination.  Borrower shall give not less than  thirty  (30)  days  prior  written  notice  to  the  Administrative  Agent  designating  the  date (which shall be a Business Day) of such termination.  Any amounts so prepaid on termination may not be reborrowed.  The Lenders Revolving Loan Commitment shall expire without further action on the part of the Lender on the Revolver Termination Date."
