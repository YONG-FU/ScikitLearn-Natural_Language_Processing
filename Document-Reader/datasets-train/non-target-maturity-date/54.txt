"Permitted Amendment means an amendment to this Agreement and the other Loan Documents, effected in connection with a Loan Modification Offer pursuant to Section 2.14, providing for an extension of the Maturity Date applicable to the Loans of the Accepting Lenders and, in connection therewith, may also provide for (a)(i) a change in the Applicable Rate with respect to the Loans of the Accepting Lenders subject to such Permitted Amendment and/or (ii) a change in the fees payable to, or the inclusion of new fees to be payable to, the Accepting Lenders in respect of such Loans, and/or (b) other changes to the terms and conditions in respect of such Loans after the Maturity Date in respect thereof prior to giving effect to any extended maturity date effected pursuant to such Loan Modification Agreement."
