"(b) Each Domestic Letter of Credit Fee shall be payable in arrears on the last Business Day of each March, June, September and December and on the Termination Date for the period from the Effective Date (or the last day on which the Domestic Letter of Credit Fee was paid with respect thereto) to the date such payment is due or, if earlier, the date on which such Letter of Credit expired or was terminated.
