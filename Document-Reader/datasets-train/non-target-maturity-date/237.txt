"The term of all Bankers Acceptances issued pursuant to any Notice of Borrowing shall be identical. The aggregate face amount of Bankers Acceptances issued pursuant to any Notice of Borrowing shall not be less than Cdn. $1,000,000 and in multiples of Cdn. $100,000 thereafter. Each Bankers Acceptance shall be dated the Borrowing Date on which it is issued, and shall be for a term of either one, two, to availability as determined by the Operating Agent), without grace, provided that in no event shall the applicable maturity of a Bankers Acceptance extend beyond the Maturity Date. There shall not be outstanding under any Credit, at any time, Accommodation by way of Bankers Acceptances, having among them, more than six (6) different maturity dates."
