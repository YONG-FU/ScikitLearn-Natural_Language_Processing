"(a) Registration  of  Notes.    Acting  solely  as  a  non-fiduciary  agent  for  the  Company  for purposes of this Section 12.5(a), Administrative Agent shall keep at its principal office a register for the registration  and  registration  of  transfers  of  the  Notes  (the  Register).    The  name  and  address  of  each Holder, each transfer thereof and the name and address of each transferee of one or more Notes shall be recorded in such Register, together with the principal amount (and related interest) of the Notes owing to, each Holder.  Prior to due presentment for registration of transfer, the Person in whose name any Note shall be registered shall be deemed and treated as the owner and Holder thereof for all purposes hereof, and Administrative Agent shall not be affected by any notice or knowledge to the contrary."
