"20.7  Right  of  Set-Off.    If  an  Event  of  Default  will  have  occurred  and  be  continuing,  each Notes Purchaser, and each of their respective Affiliates, is hereby authorized at any time and from time to time, to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, to set off and apply any and all deposits (general or special, time or demand, provisional or final, in whatever currency) at any time held and other obligations (in  whatever  currency)  at  any  time  owing  by  such Notes  Purchaser  or  any  such  Affiliate  to  or  for  the credit  or  the  account  of  Investments  or  any  other  Principal  against  any  and  all  of  the  obligations  of Investments  or  such  Principal  now  or  hereafter  existing  under  this  Agreement  or  any  other  Note Document  to  such  Notes  Purchaser  to  the  extent  such  obligations  are  due  and  payable,  irrespective  of whether or not such Notes Purchaser will have made any demand under this Agreement or any other Note Document  and  although  such  obligations  of  Investments  or  such  Principal  may  be  contingent  or unmatured or are owed to a branch or office of such Notes Purchaser different from the branch or office holding such deposit or obligated on such indebtedness.  The rights of each Notes Purchaser and their respective Affiliates under this Section 20.7 are in addition to other rights and remedies (including other rights of setoff) that such Notes Purchaser or its Affiliates may have.  Each Notes Purchaser agrees to notify Investment promptly after any such setoff and application; provided that the failure to give such notice will not affect the validity of such setoff and application."
