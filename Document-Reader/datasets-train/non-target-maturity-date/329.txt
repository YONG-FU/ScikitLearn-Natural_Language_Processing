"(a) Letters of Credit.  During the Revolving Commitment Period, subject to the terms  and  conditions  hereof,    the  L/C  Provider  agrees  to  issue  Letters  of  Credit  (or  arrange  for  the issuance  of  Letters  of  Credit  pursuant  to  the  delivery  of  a  Support  Agreement)  for  the  account  of Company  in  the  aggregate  amount  up  to  but  not  exceeding  the  Letter  of  Credit  Sublimit;  provided, (i) after giving effect to such issuance, in no event shall the Total Utilization of Revolving Commitments exceed the Revolving Commitments then in effect; (ii) after giving effect to such issuance, in no event shall the Letter of Credit Usage exceed the Letter of Credit Sublimit then in effect; and (iii) in no event shall any standby Letter of Credit have an expiration date later than the earlier of (1) thirty (30) days prior to the Revolving Commitment Termination Date, and (2) unless otherwise agreed to by the L/C Provider, the  date  which  is  one  (1)  year  from  the  date  of  issuance  of  such  standby  Letter  of  Credit;  provided, further, that (1) each Letter of Credit may, with the consent of the L/C Provider, provide for automatic renewals for one (1) year periods (which in no event shall extend beyond the date that is thirty (30) days before the Revolving Commitment Termination Date) and (2) such Letter of Credit may provide (on the initiative or at the direction of the Person issuing the Letter of Credit or the L/C Provider) that such expiry date need not be extended and such Letter of Credit may be terminated on each such annual expiration date with the giving of notice, provided, however, that (x) no such extension shall be permitted if the L/C Provider has determined that such Letter of Credit would not be permitted in its revised (as extended) form  under  the  terms  of  this  Agreement  or  the  L/C  Provider  has  determined  that  one  or  more  of  the applicable  conditions  specified  in  Section  3  or  in  this  Section  2.3  for  Credit  Extensions  is  not  then satisfied (and in respect of an extension of a Letter of Credit, such determination shall be made at the time the L/C Provider must elect or cause an election to be made to allow such extension (the L/C Extension Election Date)) and (y) the L/C Provider shall not be required to extend or cause the extension of any such  Letter  of  Credit  if  it  has  received  written  notice  from  the  Administrative  Agent  that  an  Event  of Default has occurred and is continuing at the time the L/C Provider must elect or cause to be made the election  to  allow  such  extension;  provided,  further,  in  the  event  a  Funding  Default  exists  or  any Revolving Lender is a Defaulting Lender, the L/C Provider shall not be required to issue, continue, or renew (or cause the issuance, continuance, or renewal of or provide a Support Agreement in respect of) any Letter of Credit unless the L/C Provider has entered into arrangements satisfactory to it and Company to eliminate the L/C Providers risk with respect to the participation in Letters of Credit of the Defaulting Lender, including by cash collateralizing such Defaulting Lenders Pro Rata Share of the Letter of Credit Usage.  Each of letter of credit issued or arranged for by a L/C Provider pursuant to a request by a Credit Party shall conclusively be deemed a Letter of Credit issued in accordance with this Agreement for all purposes."
