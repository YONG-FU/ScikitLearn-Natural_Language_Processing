"13.2.2  Voting Rights.  Each Lender shall retain the sole right to approve, without the  consent  of  any  Participant,  any  amendment,  waiver  or  other  modification  of  a  Loan Document other than that which forgives principal, interest or fees, reduces the stated interest rate or fees payable with respect to any Term Loan in which such Participant has an interest, postpones the Maturity Date or any date fixed for any regularly scheduled payment of principal, interest or fees on such Term Loan, or releases any Obligor or substantially all Collateral."
