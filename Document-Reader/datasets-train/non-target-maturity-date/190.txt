"The Issuer will pay or cause to be paid the principal of, premium, if any, and interest on, the Notes on the dates and in the manner provided in the Notes and this Agreement.  Principal, premium, if any, and interest will be considered paid on the date due if the Purchasers by 5:00 p.m. Eastern time on the due date receive from the Administrative Agent or from the Issuer in immediately available funds amounts  sufficient  to  pay  all  principal,  premium,  if  any,  and  interest  then  due.    All  interest  payments received by the Purchasers after 5:00 p.m. Eastern time shall be deemed received on the next succeeding Business Day and any applicable interest shall continue to accrue.  If any payment to be made by the Issuer shall come due on a day other than a Business Day, payment shall be made on the next following Business Day, and such extension of time shall be reflected in computing interest.  The Issuer will pay interest on overdue principal (including post-petition interest in any case or proceeding under any Debtor Relief Law) and on overdue installments of interest to the extent lawful, in the manner provided in the Notes."
