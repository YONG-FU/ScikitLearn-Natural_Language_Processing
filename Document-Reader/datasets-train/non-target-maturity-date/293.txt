"(a) The Collateral Agent may perform any and all of its duties and exercise its rights and powers hereunder or under any other Note Documents by or through any one or more sub-agents, delegates or attorneys-in-fact appointed by the Collateral Agent and will not be responsible for acts or omissions  of  any  such  sub-agents,  delegates  or  attorneys-in-fact  appointed  by  it  with  due  care.    The Collateral Agent and any such sub-agent may perform any and all of its duties and exercise its rights and powers by or through its respective Affiliates.  The exculpatory provisions of this Article 18 will apply to any such sub-agent and to the Affiliates of the Collateral Agent and any such sub-agent."
