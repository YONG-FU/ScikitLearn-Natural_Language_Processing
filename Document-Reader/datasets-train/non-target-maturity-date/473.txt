"""Delayed Draw Term Loan Commitment Termination Date"" means the earliest to occur  of  (i) the  date  the  Delayed  Draw  Term  Loan  Commitments  are  permanently  reduced  to zero pursuant to Section 2.01(b), (ii) the date of the termination of the Delayed Draw Term Loan Commitments pursuant to Section 2.05(a) or Section 9.01 and (iii)  5:00 p.m. (New York City time) on the later of (x) the first anniversary of the Effective Date and (y) May 31, 2015."
