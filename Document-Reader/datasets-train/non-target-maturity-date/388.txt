"Prepayment Premium means, in relation to any Loan that is prepaid or repaid during the period from (and including) the Closing Date up to (but excluding) the Termination Date, an amount equal to the  present  value  on the  prepayment  or  repayment  date  of  all  required  interest  payments (including accrued interest) due (or which would but for the prepayment or repayment  have  become  due)  on  the  principal  amount  of  the  Loans  to  be repaid  or  prepaid  from  the  date  of  the  prepayment  or  repayment  until  the Termination Date, calculated as though the interest rate then in effect were applicable until the Termination Date and including the amounts of accrued and unpaid interest to the Termination Date, in each case computed using a discount  rate  equal  to  the  US  Treasury  Rate  as  of  the  repayment  or prepayment date plus 0.50 per cent."
