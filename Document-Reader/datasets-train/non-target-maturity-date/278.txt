"3.3 Payments.    Each  payment  by  Investments  hereunder  of  the  principal  amount  of  Notes, interest  thereon,  Make-Whole  Premium,  fees,  costs,  expenses,  indemnities  and  other  Obligations  due hereunder  and  under  the  other  applicable  Note  Documents  shall  be  made  by  wire  transfer  in  Federal (same day) funds or other immediately available funds, without deduction (except as provided in Section 3.10(b)), set-off or counterclaim, to the Notes Purchaser entitled to receive such payment at such office or bank  account  as  shall  be  specified  by  such  Notes  Purchaser  from  time  to  time  by  written  notice  to Investments (it being understood that any such account shall be an account into which Investments may legally deposit funds), not later than 2:00 p.m. (New York time) on the date that such payment is due (each such payment made after such time on such due date shall be deemed to have been made on the next succeeding Business Day).  Whenever any payment hereunder or under the Notes is stated to be due on a day other than a Business Day, that payment shall be made on the immediately succeeding Business Day."
