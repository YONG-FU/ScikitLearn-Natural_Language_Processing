"Section 2.03  Repayment  of  Loans;  Evidence  of  Debt.    (a)    The  outstanding principal  of  the  Term  Loan  shall  be  repayable  in  consecutive  quarterly  installments,  in  the amount  equal  to  1.25%  times  the  principal  amount  of  the  Term  Loan  outstanding  on  the  last Business Day of each March, June, September, and December, due and payable commencing on September 30, 2015 and on the last Business Day of each September, December, March and June thereafter, ending on the Final Maturity Date; provided, however, that the last such installment shall be in the amount necessary to repay in full the unpaid principal amount of the Term Loan. The outstanding unpaid principal amount of the Term Loan, and all accrued and unpaid interest thereon, shall be due and payable on the earliest of (i) the Final Maturity Date and (ii) the date on which the Term Loan is declared due and payable pursuant to the terms of this Agreement."
