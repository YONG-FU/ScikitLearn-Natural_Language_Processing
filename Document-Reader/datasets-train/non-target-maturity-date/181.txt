"Section 9.3.  Action if Other Event of Default.  If any Event of Default (other than any Event of Default described in clauses (h) through (i) of Section 9.1 with respect to Company) shall  occur  for  any  reason,  whether  voluntary  or  involuntary,  and  be  continuing,  the Administrative Agent, upon the direction of the Required Holders, shall by notice to Company declare all or any portion of the outstanding Principal Amount of the Notes and other Obligations to be due and payable without further notice, demand or presentment, whereupon the full unpaid amount of such Notes and other Obligations that shall be so declared due and payable shall be and become immediately due and payable."
