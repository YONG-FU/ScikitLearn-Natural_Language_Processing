"Use of Proceeds.  The proceeds of the Notes will be deposited into the Issuer Account and used solely (i) to refinance or repay the Existing Credit Agreement and certain other existing indebtedness of the Note Parties set forth on Schedule 5.07 hereto, (ii) to pay for Permitted Acquisitions, (iii) to pay deferred seller payments in connection with acquisitions, (iv) to pay amounts due in respect of exercises of put and call options related to minority ownership positions in Whitney Affiliates, (v) to perform the obligations of the Issuer hereunder, including Section 6.09 and to pay fees and expenses associated with the preparation, execution and delivery of the Note Documents and the transactions contemplated thereby (which, for the avoidance of doubt, shall include, among other things, payments required to be made under the Transaction Documents), (vi) the fees and expenses associated with the execution, preparation and delivery of the transaction documents  contemplated  by  clauses (ii),  (iii)  and  (iv)  above  and  the  consummation  of  the transactions contemplated thereby and (vii) in the case of Declined Proceeds, for general corporate purposes.  All such proceeds shall be used as otherwise contemplated hereby and within a reasonably prompt period of time after drawing hereunder."
