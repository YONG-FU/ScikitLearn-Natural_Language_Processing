"(c) Continuation  of  Loans.    Notwithstanding  any  other  provision  of  this Agreement, the Borrower shall not be entitled to (a) request, or to elect to continue any  borrowing  of  Eurodollar  Loans  if  the  Interest  Period  requested  with  respect thereto would end after the Maturity Date, or (b) subject to Section 3.03 and Section 5.05, convert any Eurodollar Loan into an ABR Loan."
