"In the case of an Event of Default specified in clause (a)(viii) or (a)(ix) of Section 8.1, all outstanding  Notes  will  become  due  and  payable  immediately  without  further  action  or  notice.    If  any other Event of Default occurs and is continuing, the Majority Purchasers may direct the Administrative Agent to, and thereupon the Administrative Agent shall, declare all the Notes to be due and payable by written notice to the Issuer, whereupon the Notes shall become due and payable.  Upon the effectiveness of an Event of Default specified in clause (a)(viii) or (a)(ix) of Section 8.1 or any such declaration, the outstanding principal and premium (if any) of,  and all accrued but unpaid interest, fees and expenses on, the Notes will become due and payable immediately."
